Delhi

All about Delhi and around

Delhi, India’s capital territory, is a massive metropolitan area in the country’s north. In Old Delhi, a neighborhood dating to the 1600s, stands the imposing Mughal-era Red Fort, a symbol of India, and the sprawling Jama Masjid mosque, whose courtyard accommodates 25,000 people. Nearby is Chandni Chowk, a vibrant bazaar filled with food carts, sweets shops and spice stalls. Delhi is famous for its heritage sites, featuring UNESCO Heritage sites like Red Fort, Humayun's Tomb, Jama Masjid and Qutub Minar. Some of the other popular tourist destinations in Delhi are Old Fort, the famous ISKCON temple
